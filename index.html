About Me

I'm Isaac Chikwere Kasenjere, born on 9 January, a Hospitality business strategist, TEDx Global Speaker, author, and Ventriloqust driven by a mission to transform how the world experiences education, wellness and tourism.

My talks explore the intersections of creativity, leadership, and service showing how innovation in hospitality can drive community wellness and sustainabe growth.
